Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my notes?
To upload your notes, go to the Upload page, drag and drop your file or use the browse option, fill in the metadata, and click Upload.
How do I search for notes?
Use the search bar on the Dashboard or the Search page to filter notes by keywords. The results update automatically.
What file formats are supported?
We support common formats such as PDF, DOCX, JPEG, and PNG. Check our upload guidelines for more details.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the Report button on the File Details page to flag content. Our team will review your report and take action.
How is my reputation calculated?
Your reputation is based on factors such as uploads, downloads, and ratings from other users. Check your profile for detailed insights.
